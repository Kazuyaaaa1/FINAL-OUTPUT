About Us

We created this site to promote informed, practical food choices at home and in school. Our goal is to make food safety, nutrition, and responsible consumption easy to understand and apply every day.

Our Mission

Empower students and families with simple, science‑based tips: safe handling, storage, hygiene, and mindful eating. We also celebrate Filipino flavors like Mang Tomas—enjoyed responsibly.

Contact

Have suggestions or corrections? Reach out to the project team at school project group. This site is for educational purposes only.
